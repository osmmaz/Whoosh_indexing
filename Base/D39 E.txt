This study explained the attitude of Shaykh Muhammad al Ghazali towards
the claims of the Jewish orientalist Goldziher from Islam. This study is
devided into three chapters. The first one highlights the biography of Sheikh
Muhammad Al Ghazali and his Islamic production and his attitude towards
orientalism. The second chapter highlights the biography of the Orientalist
Goldzhier and his attitude towards Islam, the last chapter focuses on the
claims of Goldzhier on the Islamic doctrine and the responses of Sheikh
Mohammed al Ghazali. The study accentuated Sheikh Mohammed al 
Ghazali's efforts in defending Islam and Islamic culture, and recommended
the importance of standing up to the Orientalist thought and confronting its
dangers.
